Welcome to Chucktowne Charters

Our Mission

ChuckTowne Charters is a locally owned fishing charter company in the waters of Charleston, South Carolina specifically targeting Tarpon. We strive to provide the ultimate fishing experience for anyone who is looking to chase the Elusive Silver king. Every single time we are out on the water we look to educate clients and protect our waters. Frequently Asked Questions Do we need to bring anything? It is highly suggested you bring polarized sunglasses, Sunscreen, Snacks, and anything else you might need for a long day on the water. Do we need fishing experience? Prior experience is always good but you don’t have to have ever even stepped foot on a boat. We will get you comfortable and ready to fight the fish of a life time. Do we need a fishing license? In the state of South Carolina as long as the vessel is registered through Coast Guard you do not need a license.
