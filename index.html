Welcome to Chucktowne Charters

Our Mission

ChuckTown Charters is a locally owned fishing charter company in the waters of Charleston, South Carolina specifically targeting Tarpon. We strive to provide the ultimate fishing experience for anyone who is looking to chase the Elusive Silver king. Every single time we are out on the water we look to educate clients and protect our waters.
